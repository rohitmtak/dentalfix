Why should you get Invisalign?

Improve your bite

Straight teeth help an individual to effectively bite, chew and speak. Straight teeth contribute to healthy teeth and gums ensuring a long life for your teeth.

Better aesthetics

Teeth that work better also tend to look better. An attractive smile is a wonderful asset.

Unlock Your Best Smile

Say goodbye to crowding and overbite with Invisalign, not only enhancing your smile but also improving your overall wellbeing, confidence, and self-esteem.

Invisalign is the gold standard of clear aligners.

Completing the picture-perfect smile, we provide teeth whitening treatments both during and after your Invisalign journey.

Reduce susceptibility to decay and injury

You may be surprised to learn that aligned teeth are less prone to decay and injury.

Visibly invisible

Your Invisalign clear aligners are virtually invisible! Continue eating all your favorite foods without anything getting stuck in braces.

Our Transparent Process

Step 1

Free Consultation

Schedule a casual conversation at one of our friendly dental practices to see if Invisalign is perfect for you.

Step 2

Treatment Planning

Using our iTero 3-D scanner, we map out a customised plan for your smile.

Step 3

Aligner Creation

Insight-driven technology produces a series of custom-fitted aligners to gradually shift your teeth.

Step 4

Progress Monitoring

We monitor and support you at every visit to ensure optimal progress.

Step 5

Retainers and Whitening

Finish with complimentary retainers and whitening to maintain your new smile.
