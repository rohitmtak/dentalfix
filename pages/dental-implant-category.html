Implants for 3–4 Missing Teeth

Restore your smile and function with a fixed bridge supported by dental implants. This solution is stable, aesthetic, and preserves your healthy teeth.

Treatment Overview

How it Works

2–3 implants are placed to anchor a custom bridge. This restores chewing ability and prevents bone loss from missing teeth.

Benefits

Preserves Teeth

No need to grind down healthy teeth for a bridge.

Natural Look

Custom bridge blends with your smile.

Better Chewing

Eat confidently with a stable, fixed solution.

Prevents Bone Loss

Implants stimulate bone and prevent shrinkage.

Materials Used

Implant Materials

Titanium or zirconia implants for strength and biocompatibility.

Bridge Materials

Bridges use ceramic materials for durability and natural appearance.

Procedure Steps

Consultation

Personalized treatment planning and 3D imaging.

Implant Placement

Precise placement of dental implants.

Healing

Implants fuse with bone over several months.

Bridge Attachment

Custom bridge is secured to the implants.

Full Arch Implants

Regain a complete smile with a full arch of implant-supported teeth.

All-on-4 Implants

A fixed, full-arch solution using just four implants for maximum stability.
